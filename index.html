What is Udhyam Registration Certificate?

The Udhyam Registration Certificate is an official government document that recognizes your business as a Micro, Small, or Medium Enterprise (MSME). This certificate provides access to numerous benefits and schemes offered by the Indian government.

  • Collateral-free loans from banks
  • Subsidies on patent registration
  • Preference in government tenders
  • Concession on electricity bills
  • Tax rebates and exemptions
  • Protection against delayed payments
